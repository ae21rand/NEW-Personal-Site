Siena Beverage Institute

At the Siena Beverage Institute, I research the history, production, and tasting notes of a variety of beverages and help lead educational tastings for students, faculty, and alumni. During the summer we also work with external partners on various projects. My focus is on the visual and design aspect of marketing and content creation.
